About

Students will get familiar with KUBO and the TagTiles® by using their own body to act out the commands printed on the movement tiles. They will build an understanding of sequences and algorithms by laying down movement TagTiles® and programming KUBO to do the actions printed on them. In later exercises, they will explore functions.

KUBOs (incl.)

Activity map (incl.)

Pencils

Setup

1. Make copies of worksheets 1.1, 1.2, 1.4, 1.5, and 1.8 — one for each student.
2. Make sure all KUBOs have been fully charged before beginning.
3. Find an appropriate place to do the exercises. KUBO can be used on a table or the floor, but the surface must be level and clean.
4. Help students find the TagTiles® and activity map they will need.

Goals and objectives

By the end of this section, the students should know how to use concrete, visual, and symbolic representations.
By the end of this section, students should be able to:
Understand how movement TagTiles® work. Make routes for KUBO to follow on the activity map. Explain what a function is. Make a function.
